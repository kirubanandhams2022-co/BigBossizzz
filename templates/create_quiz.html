  • Excel

Excel Format (.xlsx, .xls)

Columns required:

  • question - The question text
  • option_a, option_b, option_c, option_d - Answer options
  • correct_answer - The correct option text
Example: Create a spreadsheet with headers and fill in your questions row by row.
Word Document (.docx)

Format your document with:

  • Questions numbered (1., 2., 3., etc.) or marked with "Q:"
  • Options labeled A), B), C), D) or a), b), c), d)
  • Mark correct answers with * or (correct)
Example: 1. What is 2+2? A) 3 B) *4 C) 5 D) 6
CSV Format

Column structure:

Question, Option1, Option2, Option3, Option4, CorrectAnswer, Points

Example:
"What is 2+2?","3","4","5","6","4","1"
Text Format (.txt)

Structure:

Q: Question text
A) Option 1
B) Option 2  
C) *Correct Option
D) Option 4

Q: Next question...
Use * to mark the correct answer
Pro Tip: Excel format is recommended for bulk uploads as it provides the most structured approach and supports unlimited questions.
